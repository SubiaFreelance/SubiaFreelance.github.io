Common questions.

What if I don't have a resume at all?
That's what the CV Writing package is for. The guided questionnaire walks you through everything I need. I take your answers and build a professional CV — well-structured, clearly written, and ready to use.
I already have a resume. Is it worth paying you to redo it?
If you wrote it yourself or had it done by a service that doesn't understand your industry — probably yes. Most CVs I see bury the strongest material, describe tasks instead of outcomes, and miss the framing that makes a hiring manager stop and read closely. I restructure around what actually matters to the person making the decision.
How do revisions work?
I send you a draft. You reply by email with what you'd like changed — plain text, screenshots, marked-up notes, whatever's easiest. I make updates and return a new draft within 24 hours. No calls needed.
Do I need any technical skills for the website?
No. I handle everything — design, hosting, domain configuration. You provide your career information and preferences. I do the rest.
Is the hosting really free?
Yes. Your site is hosted on GitHub Pages — a free service with no bandwidth limits, no ads, and no subscriptions. If you want a custom domain (like yourname.com), that's about $12/year. I configure it for you.
Can I update the website myself later?
Yes. I'll show you how to make simple text changes. For larger updates, I offer revision sessions at $60/hour.
